Our Services

These are the steps we follow to serve food to people in need from volunteers.

Food Collection

We collect food which may be wasted from hotels.Food being produced in excess is a common thing in many hotels.This food is then given to the needy people after making sure that it is in consumable condition

Food Distribution

We also maintain logistics to distribute the basic ammenities required by the needed people. These distributions are made by volunteers thus ensuring that the required items reach the correct place.

Donation

We are a Non-profit organization ,thus donation can help us to greatly extent our reach.
